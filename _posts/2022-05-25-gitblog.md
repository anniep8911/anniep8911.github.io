 ## 미리보기
 미리보기를 하기위해서는 아래와 같이 번들을 설치해야한다.<br>
 ```cmd
 bundle install
 ```
 <img src="https://blogger.googleusercontent.com/img/a/AVvXsEhb8F9Q2F1nRffK5bNLofDhsE2i510O38gjl8BHy6k3aG4uyLRLxxii16aOhIp08gXfOaZYIt9jAf6yFegKttKOxlgO0qSx7RD6N5pKqKFoamVRhH7fSF1V4E7UxblHesoGvbXieuE6uvva0T4tTBbWiWNRZDC8vLK0SFr-DRVuVINDkwREVpJMiYGU"/>

```cmd
bundle exec jekyll serve
```
